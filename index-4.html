How to play

Each player putts from their own line toward the far end zone. One putt each per round, opener first.

In the end zone → +2 points.

Off the mat (too hard) → −2 points.

Short, on the mat → no points yet — it comes down to who's closest.

After both putts, the round scores like this (opener across the top, responder down the side):

zoneshortoff
zone0 / 0+2 / 0+2 / −2
short0 / +2closest +1+1 / −2
off−2 / +2−2 / +1−2 / −2

Both in the zone cancel out. If neither scores a zone, the ball closest to its target zone takes +1; the other gets nothing (no negative).

First to the target score wins. Scores can go negative. The opener is the same player every round — there's an edge and a risk to going first.

Controls: hold the board (or the space bar) to charge the power gauge, and release to putt. The longer you hold, the harder you hit — let go too late and you'll fly off the mat.
